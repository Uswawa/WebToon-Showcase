Top Categories

Action / System

Centers on overpowered main characters who gain video-game-like abilities, level up, and conquer portals or dungeons.

Murim (Martial Arts)

Rooted in traditional Korean and Chinese martial arts, featuring Jianghu, sect politics, and legendary weaponry.

Regression / Reincarnation

Characters get a second chance at life, traveling back in time or reincarnating into their past selves or a fantasy world to change their fate.

Otome Isekai

Typically features female leads who are transported into the world of a novel or video game, often as a villainess, royalty, or supporting character.

School Life / Delinquent

Set in modern South Korea, these stories follow high schoolers navigating gang conflicts, bullying, and street fighting.

Romance / Comedy

Focuses on relationship building, workplace romance, or historical courtships, frequently utilizing witty dialogue.

Featured Web Novels

The Greatest Estate Developer

Suho Kim, a civil engineering student, transmigrates into the body of Lloyd Frontera—a lazy, indebted, and reviled nobleman from a fantasy novel. Armed with modern engineering knowledge and a giant hamster contract, Suho uses civil engineering and magic to save his family from ruin, building prosperous cities.

Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling is a dark fantasy action series where "Hunters" battle monsters emerging from dimensional portals. It follows Sung Jin-woo, humanity's weakest hunter, who gains the impossible ability to level up. He transforms into an unstoppable "Shadow Monarch," using necromancy to build an army of the dead.

Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint

Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint (ORV) follows Kim Dokja, an average office worker and the sole reader of a niche web novel. When reality suddenly transforms into the apocalyptic setting of the story he has followed for a decade, he must use his unique foreknowledge to survive and alter the plot.

The Beginning After The End

The Beginning After the End is a popular, epic fantasy series that follows King Grey, a cold, powerful, but unfulfilled ruler in a martial-arts-driven world. After dying mysteriously, he is reincarnated into a vibrant, magical new world as a baby named Arthur Leywin. Retaining his past wisdom and combat instincts, he sets out to correct his past mistakes and protect his new loved ones.

Pick Me Up, Infinite Gacha

Pick Me Up: Infinite Gacha follows a top-ranked Earth gamer who is mysteriously sucked into a notoriously difficult mobile gacha game. Transported into the body of a weak 1-star rookie, he must orchestrate survival, lead a ragtag party up a 100-floor tower, and defeat the higher-dimensional player acting as his "Master".
